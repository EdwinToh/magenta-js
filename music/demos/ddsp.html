DDSP

DDSP lets you combine the interpretable structure of classical DSP elements (such as filters, oscillators, reverberation, etc.) with the expressivity of deep learning

Initialize

const ddsp = new mm.DDSP();

Extract Audio Features

ddsp.getAudioFeatures(audioBuffer)

Using SPICE, we extract pitches from an AudioBuffer. We also extract loudness at the same time.

Synthesize

ddsp.synthesize(model, audioFeatures)

Synthesize your audio into one of the 4 preset models ('violin', 'trumpet', 'tenor_saxophone', 'flute')
